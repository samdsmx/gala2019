Join us on Saturday, October 19, 2019  to celebrate the 2nd Annual Gala of omegaUp "Coding Latam", our main fundraising event. We support and help prosper future Engineers in Latin America and you can be part of it!

We can't achieve our mission without your help. The gala will be filled with autumn magic that will include a sponsor reception, dinner, entertainment, and silent auctions.

2010

Following a strong desire to help our community and
a firm belief that the language should not be a barrier to develop a talent in software engineering, we built a free online platform where teachers can design coding challenges and programming contests for students to solve with content in Spanish.

2011

omegaUp became the official platform of the Mexican Olympiad in Informatics. Our platform is used through all the different stages of the Olympiad until 4 students are chosen to represent México at the International Olympiad in Informatics.

2014

We are proud to support this great competition and we are happy to contribute to the dramatic improvement in performance of México at the International Olympiad in Informatics. In just a short period of time México has doubled its bronze medals, tripled the number of silver medals, and got its very first gold medal in 2014.

2017

omegaUp is a 501(c)(3) non-profit with the mission of building the talent pipeline of Software Engineering in America Latina.

We started hosting the Peruvian Olympiad in Informatics

2018
